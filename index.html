About The Forgiveness Machine

The Forgiveness Machine is a small digital space built for heavy hearts. Here, you write what’s been hurting you- something you want to forgive, or something you hope to be forgiven for.
When you press Forgive, the words disappear.
Not because they were unimportant- but because you chose to release them. This simple act of writing, sharing, and letting go helps your mind breathe again.
No judgments. No records. No memories kept. Just you, your feelings, and a moment of peace.
You don’t have to carry everything alone. Sometimes, healing starts with one gentle click.
